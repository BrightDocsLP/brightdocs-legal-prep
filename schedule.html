Before You Book

BrightDocs consultations are designed to help you understand what documents you may need and how the preparation process works. Consultations may last up to 90 minutes, depending on your needs.

The consultation fee must be paid in advance to secure your appointment. If you decide to move forward with BrightDocs, the consultation fee will be credited toward your document preparation cost.

Appointments may be available at the office or through a home visit, depending on your location and scheduling needs.
