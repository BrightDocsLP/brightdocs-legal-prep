About Cindylou

BrightDocs Legal Prep, LLC (BrightDocs) was founded by Cindylou Richardson, a proud Arizonan whose roots run deep in the community. Cindylou understands the value of hard work, family, and getting the job done, qualities that guide everything BrightDocs stands for today.

Married with a blended family of six, she knows firsthand how important it is to have someone you can rely on when life's paperwork starts to feel overwhelming. That's why she built BrightDocs: to make legal document preparation affordable, accessible, and handled with care and commitment for everyday people who simply need assistance.

Experience

With more than eighteen years of hands-on legal experience, Cindylou Richardson has worked in both the public and private sectors, including with the Maricopa County Clerk of the Superior Court and privately held companies. She is an Arizona Certified Legal Document Preparer through the Arizona Supreme Court. Cindylou also holds a Paralegal Studies Certificate from Phoenix College and a bachelor's degree in human communications from Arizona State University. Her extensive background in legal processing and document preparation, combined with her passion for helping people navigate important life transitions, inspired her to start BrightDocs. Through BrightDocs, everyday Arizonans can find confidence and trusted support with their legal paperwork.

At BrightDocs, your legal needs matter. Whether you are planning your estate, filing family court documents, or setting up a new business, Cindylou is committed to providing clear communication, accessible services, and affordable legal document assistance.

Our Promise

We promise to provide every client with honest communication, professional legal documents, and reliable service, all at a fair price.
