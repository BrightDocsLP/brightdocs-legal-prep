About Cindylou

BrightDocs Legal Prep, LLC (BrightDocs) was founded by Cindylou Richardson, a proud Arizonan whose roots run deep in the community. Cindylou understands the value of hard work, family, and getting the job done, qualities that guide everything BrightDocs stands for today.

Married with a blended family of six, she knows firsthand how important it is to have someone you can rely on when life's paperwork starts to feel overwhelming. That's why she built BrightDocs: to make legal document preparation affordable, accurate, and accessible for everyday people who simply need assistance.

Experience

With over a decade of hands-on legal experience, Cindylou Richardson has worked in both private law firms and public offices, including the Maricopa County Superior Court and Lewis Roca PLLC. She holds a Paralegal Studies Certificate from Phoenix College and is a commissioned Notary Signing Agent through the National Notary Association. Her extensive background in legal document preparation, combined with a passion for helping people navigate life's important transitions, inspired her to start BrightDocs Legal Prep, LLC. Through BrightDocs Legal Prep, LLC, everyday Arizonans can find clarity, confidence, and trusted support in their legal paperwork.

At BrightDocs, your legal needs matter. Whether you're filing family documents, planning your estate, or setting up a new business, Cindylou and her team are here to make the legal process clear, accessible, and affordable.

Our Promise

We promise to provide every client with honest communication, professional legal documents, and reliable service, all at a fair price.
