Key Services

We provide professional document preparation for a variety of legal needs, including:

  • Estate Planning Documents - Trust and Will packages, including the Will, all three Arizona Powers of Attorney for Personal Property and Affairs, Healthcare, Mental Healthcare, Deeds to Trust, and Beneficiary Deeds.
  • Business Documents - LLC Creation with the Arizona Corporation Commission and Employer Identification Number (EIN) with the Internal Revenue Service (IRS).
  • Real Estate Documents - Property Deed Transfers, Warranty Deeds, Beneficiary Deeds, Affidavit of Value, Affidavit of Affixture, and Vehicle Transfer on Death beneficiary designations.
  • Probate Law Documents - Small Estate Affidavit, Informal Probate, Guardianship, Conservatorship, and Dependency Actions.
  • Family Law Documents - Divorce, Separation, Establish Paternity, Child Support, Parenting Time, and Child Support/Parenting Time Modifications.
  • Civil Law Documents - Name Change and Correct Birth Certificate.

BrightDocs Legal Prep, LLC is not a law firm and cannot provide legal advice.

How It Works

We keep things simple and straightforward:

  1. Schedule an Appointment - Meet in the office or request a home visit.
  2. Share Your Information - We gather the details needed for your documents.
  3. We Prepare Your Documents - Professionally completed and ready to sign or file.
  4. You Review and Approve - We finalize everything for accuracy and satisfaction.

Personalized Service

Your convenience comes first. You're welcome to meet with our legal document preparer in our office for a private consultation, or if it's more convenient, we can come to you. BrightDocs offers flexible appointment options designed to make the process as easy and comfortable as possible.

Consultation Fee

A consultation fee is required to schedule your appointment. We aim to educate and provide options with a no-pressure atmosphere.

If you decide to have your documents prepared with BrightDocs, the full consultation fee will be applied toward the cost of your document preparation within a set period of time negotiated with you.

This ensures you receive professional, personalized service and a clear path forward from the very first meeting.
