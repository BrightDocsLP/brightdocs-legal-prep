Key Services

We provide professional document preparation for a variety of legal needs, including:

  • Estate Planning Documents - Trust and Will packages including Last Will and Testament, Arizona Powers of Attorney (Financial, Healthcare, and Mental Healthcare,) Living Will, Warranty Deed/Beneficiary Deed, and other related documents.
  • Business Documents - LLC Creation with the Arizona Corporation Commission and Employer Identification Number (EIN) with the Internal Revenue Service (IRS).
  • Real Estate Documents - Property Deed Transfers, Warranty Deeds, Beneficiary Deeds, Affidavit of Value, Affidavit of Affixture, Vehicle Transfer on Death Beneficiary Designations, Promissory Notes, and Deeds of Trust.
  • Probate Law Documents - Informal Probate, Guardianship, Conservatorship, Dependency Actions, and Small Estate Affidavit.
  • Family Law Documents - Uncontested Divorce, Separation, Child Support, & Parenting Time; and Uncontested Child Support & Parenting Time Modifications.
  • Civil Law Documents - Name Change and Correct Birth Certificate.

BrightDocs Legal Prep, LLC is a Certified Legal Document Preparation business. We are not a law firm and cannot provide legal advice or legal representation.

How It Works

Working with BrightDocs is simple:

  1. Schedule an Appointment - As a mobile legal document preparation company, we meet you at your home, office, online through Zoom, or another convenient location.
  2. Share Your Information - We gather the details needed for your documents at the appointment.
  3. We Prepare Your Documents - We professionally prepare your documents using the information you provide.
  4. You Review and Approve - Review your completed documents before they are signed or filed.
