Key Services

We provide professional document preparation for a variety of legal needs, including:

  • Estate Planning Documents - Trust and Will packages, including the Last Will and Testament, Arizona Powers of Attorney (Financial, Healthcare, and Mental Healthcare,) Living Will, Special Warranty Deed, Beneficiary Deed, and other related documents.
  • Business Documents - Limited Liability Company (LLC) Formation with the Arizona Corporation Commission and an Employer Identification Number (EIN) with the Internal Revenue Service (IRS).
  • Real Estate Documents - Property Deed Transfers, such as: Special Warranty Deeds, Beneficiary Deeds, Affidavit of Value, Affidavit of Affixture, Vehicle Transfer on Death Beneficiary Designations, Promissory Notes, and Deeds of Trust.
  • Probate Law Documents - Informal Probate & Small Estate Affidavits for deceased persons, Guardianship, Conservatorship, & Dependency Actions for vulnerable adults and minor children.
  • Family Law Documents - Divorce, Separation, Child Support, & Parenting Time; and Child Support & Parenting Time Modifications.
  • Civil Law Documents - Name Change of an Adult, Minor, or Family.

BrightDocs Legal Prep, LLC is a certified legal document preparation business. We are not attorneys and cannot provide legal advice or legal representation.

How It Works

Working with BrightDocs is simple:

  1. Schedule an Appointment - As a mobile legal document preparation company, we meet you at your home or office, online via Zoom, or other convenient location.
  2. Share Your Information - At your appointment, we provide general factual information about legal processes and options, then gather the details needed to prepare your documents.
  3. We Prepare Your Documents - We professionally prepare your documents using the information you provide.
  4. You Review and Approve - You will have the opportunity to review your completed documents before they are signed.

Personalized Service
